E7 RTA Bot

Welcome!

I hope you have fun using this tool. It is an AI-based predictor that can predict the match result before it even starts!

If you encounter any bugs or have any suggestions regarding the new features, feel free to reach me on Discord: tokarinrika#3954

Important: If you don't see the result right away, please wait a few seconds!

Prediction result details

  • Win-rates: Your win-rate and opponent's win-rate is displayed. Note that win-rate is a relative measure of your picks' strength against the opponent's!
  • Colors: Ranging from dark purple to bright yellow, indicating the potential contribution of the hero to the match!
